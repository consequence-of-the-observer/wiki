Armors of the Divine

The Brotherhood of Divinity

In tales of old it was once said
the old world was not born, but built
a cosmic force known as The Divines entered this space-time
facisnated by the over-abundance of different energies
The Divines used their power to harness the various powers
controlling such raw energy is dangerous, but once channeled
the energy could be used to manufacture dimensions
and thus the Realm Crystal was born
built upon a virtually infinite collection of worlds
but alas...

Overtime the divines disapeared, but their tales lived on across the fragmented realities.
Temples built in their name stores the mystical armor they wore.
but beware adventurers, for these temples are filled with disaster.
